It’s 5 a.m. He says goodbye to his family and boards the train.
Blues of leaving home mixes with the excitement of new city and a dream job.

An hour passes on scrolling reels as he tried to distract from his anxiety.
He puts his phone to charge and turns to the seat window.

The sunrise lights up the mountains and a small town drifts by.
He takes off his headphones and decides to simply listen.

  • Clap to cheer the birds
  • Whistle at the clouds
  • Hiss at the mountains
  • Greet the travelers
